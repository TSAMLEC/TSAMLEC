MLEC's TSA Chapter


Mission Statement: The Technology Student Association enhances personal development, leadership, and career opportunities in STEM, whereby members apply and integrate these concepts through intracurricular activities, competitions, and related programs.

The TSA chapter at Morris Hills High School prides itself on being a chapter that is almost entirely student-driven. As we are a relatively new chapter, we have won few awards. Students meet on their own time, and are basically responsible for themselves.
Because our chapter encourages students to do all the work, it is a team that fosters communication, self-motivation, and management, among other skills.
We are a rather small chapter, consisting of just 17 students, but our members have the work ethic to get things done.
