Our school, Miami Lakes Educational Center, has the Cambridge Academy available with an AICE curriculum.


Engineering

Purpose is to give students the foundation of knowledge and technological experiences oriented in the application of engineering, how it affects our lives and the importance of choosing our future careers. The program focuses on work-related skills and stresses the understanding and demonstration of technical machines, instruments, materials, processes, and systems in the engineering business/industry. Students are able to earn multiple certifications that include: Certified Solidworks Associate (CSWA), Certified SolidWorks Professional (CSWP), and the Mastercam Associate certification.


Clubs

Technology Student Association (TSA), VEX Robotics and Battle Bots, and Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) are all hosted by the leader of the Cambridge Engineering section, Paul Kynerd. Our work area contains a lab area located at the front of the classroom armed with computers in a 3-row setup. The back is the hands-on area where students get to work and demonstrate the physical aspects of the engineering process.
